Smart Email Signature

Dynamic, executive-grade HTML signature with AI-themed visuals, animated accents, and dark mode support.

Setup Guide

1

Personalize Your Signature

Open signature.html in any text editor. Search for <!-- EDIT --> comments to find every field you should customize: name, title, email, website, location, metrics, social links, and the AI insight quote.

2

Add Your Photo

Replace the placeholder SVG avatar with your headshot. Find the avatar section (search for "Replace with your hosted headshot") and swap in an <img> tag pointing to a hosted image URL. Recommended size: 72x72px, hosted on a reliable CDN or your company server.

3

Install in Your Email Client

Open signature.html in a browser, then select all (Ctrl/Cmd+A) and copy (Ctrl/Cmd+C). Paste directly into your email client's signature editor:

Gmail Settings > See all settings > Signature > Paste
Outlook Settings > Mail > Compose > Email signature > Paste
Apple Mail Preferences > Signatures > Create new > Paste
Thunderbird Account Settings > Signature text > Use HTML > Paste source
4

Keep It Dynamic

Update these elements quarterly to keep your signature fresh and relevant:

  • AI Insight quote — rotate a new data/AI insight each quarter
  • Metrics dashboard — update with latest achievement numbers
  • CTA banner — swap in your next speaking event, publication, or initiative
  • Status indicator — set to "Available" / "Traveling" / "In Conference"
